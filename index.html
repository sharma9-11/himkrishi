Welcome to HimKrishi: Empowering Himachal's Farmers

Farming in the hills of Himachal Pradesh is a proud tradition, but it comes with unique challenges. From navigating different elevation belts and unpredictable weather to managing specific apple varieties and complex spray schedules, precision is everything. That is where HimKrishi comes in.

Built specifically for the hardworking farmers of Himachal, HimKrishi is a comprehensive digital companion designed to bring expert agricultural guidance directly to your smartphone. Whether you are managing a traditional orchard or exploring modern high-density planting, HimKrishi bridges the gap between generations of farming wisdom and cutting-edge technology.

What Makes HimKrishi Different?

  • Highly Localized Profiles: Agriculture in the mountains is not one-size-fits-all. HimKrishi tailors its guidance based on your specific village, elevation belt, apple varieties, and the age of your trees.
  • BaghbanAI Assistant: Meet your 24/7 digital agronomy guide. Powered by advanced AI, BaghbanAI understands the official spray schedules and local conditions of Himachal. You can ask it questions about crop diseases, specific chemical recommendations, and weather alerts, and it will give you immediate, contextual advice.

  • Privacy & Trust: We respect your livelihood and your data. Your orchard details are kept secure, and our transparent privacy policies ensure you are always in control of your information.

Our mission is simple: to help you increase productivity, reduce crop loss, and make informed decisions that protect your farm.
